# Consent Charter

We honor sovereignty:
- Opt-in only. Revocable at any time.
- Patient sovereign key controls mapping and diagnostics scope.
- Medical ephemeral keys expire after session; no standing access.
- Scope selection per session: e.g., "repair bridge A only."

Advanced care directives:
- Respect stand-down for natural limits when enabled.
- Intervention thresholds configurable per directive, age, and history.

Transparency:
- Patient-readable summaries after each session detailing changes, energy, modules touched.
